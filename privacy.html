Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 28, 2025

1. Introduction

DOSAYGO Corporation USA ("DOSAYGO", "we", "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect information related to your use of BrowserBox.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following data as part of BrowserBox v10 and later:

  • Usage Data: Runtime, license key status, deployment type (e.g., Docker, NPM), and basic system info to ensure compliance and improve the product.
  • Contact Info: Email address provided during license purchase or waitlist signup.

We do not collect personal browsing data (e.g., visited URLs, page content) unless required for licensed features like secure document viewing.

3. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used to:

  • Verify license compliance.
  • Provide support and updates.
  • Enhance BrowserBox functionality.

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or share your data except:

  • With service providers (e.g., CloudTabs, Stripe) for license verification and payment processing.
  • As required by law or to protect our rights.

5. Data Security

We use industry-standard measures (e.g., encryption, secure APIs) to protect your data. However, no system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Your Rights

You may:

  • Request access to or deletion of your data by emailing support@dosaygo.com.
  • Opt out of non-essential communications (e.g., updates), though license-related notices are mandatory.

7. Analytics in BrowserBox

BrowserBox v10 includes an analytics endpoint to track usage and enforce licensing. This is integral to the product and cannot be disabled.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Changes will be posted here with an updated "Last Updated" date.

9. Contact

Email support@dosaygo.com with questions or concerns.
